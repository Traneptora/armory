***Wondrous item, common***

This bullseye lantern contains an Eberron dragonshard that sheds light comparable to that produced by a *[continual flame](https://grimoire.thebombzen.xyz/spells/continual-flame)* spell. An everbright lantern sheds light in a 120-foot cone; the closest 60 feet is bright light, and the farthest 60 feet is dim light.
