***Potion, uncommon***

When you drink this potion, you can cast the *[animal friendship](https://grimoire.thebombzen.xyz/spells/animal-friendship)* spell (save DC 13) for 1 hour at will. Agitating this muddy liquid brings little bits into view: a fish scale, a hummingbird tongue, a cat claw, or a squirrel hair.
