***Staff, rare (requires attunemnt by a bard, cleric, or druid)***

This staff has 10 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more of its charges to cast one of the following spells from it, using your spell save DC and spellcasting ability modifier: *[cure wounds](https://grimoire.thebombzen.xyz/spells/cure-wounds)* (1 charge per spell level, up to 4th), *[lesser restoration](https://grimoire.thebombzen.xyz/spells/lesser-restoration)* (2 charges). or *[mass cure wounds](https://grimoire.thebombzen.xyz/spells/mass-cure-wounds)* (5 charges).

The staff regains 1d6 + 4 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20. On a 1. the staff vanishes in a flash of light, lost forever.
