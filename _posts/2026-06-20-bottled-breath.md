***Potion, uncommon***

This bottle contains a breath of elemental air. When you inhale it, you either exhale it or hold it.

If you exhale the breath, you gain the effect of the *[gust of wind](<https://grimoire.traneptora.xyz/spells/gust-of-wind>)* spell. If you hold the breath, you don’t need to breathe for 1 hour, though you can end this benefit early (for example, to speak). Ending it early doesn’t give you the benefit of exhaling the breath.

*(Armory Note: This item does not specify the saving throw DC for Gust of Wind. Uncommon items typically have a DC somewhere between 13 and 15.)*
